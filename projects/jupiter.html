Jupiter will be a webapp that keeps track of all your achievements and helps you design both a resume and CV.

A resume can only contain the highlights of your achievements, skills, experiences, etc. while a CV should encapsulate all of these events. Jupiter will allow you to take all of these experiences and easily design both a resume and CV. The project is named after jupter notebooks, as each item on your resume/CV will act like a cell.
